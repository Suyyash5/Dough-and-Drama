Welcome to Dough & Drama.

SLICES & STORIES

Dough & Drama started with one simple idea: good food tastes even better when shared with the right people. Hidden in the lively streets of Chandigarh, our space was built for late-night cravings, chaotic friend groups, first dates, heartbreak recoveries, and those “just one more slice” conversations that somehow last for hours.

Every pizza here is made with fresh dough, bold flavors, ridiculous amounts of cheese, and a little bit of personality. From classic wood-fired favorites to experimental toppings that probably shouldn’t work but somehow do, we believe food should never feel boring.
